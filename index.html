Eligible consumers may get ACP service from any participating provider. Your ACP benefits may be transferred to another provider at any time. Lifeline subscribers may continue their existing Lifeline services without enrolling in the ACP and may choose to take ACP benefits from a service provider other than their existing Lifeline provider.
